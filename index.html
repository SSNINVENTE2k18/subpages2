Accommodation



Instructions

  • All members of a team should register for accommodation individually.

  • Accommodation will be provided on a sharing basis. However, large teams or teams from the same college may be put up in the same rooms.

  • It is mandatory to bring your college ID card

  • Accommodation Charge : Rs.100/- per day per person.

  • Participants will be charged on 24-hour basis, that is you will be charged from the day of check-in till the day you check out. If any delay is made in checking out, caution deposit will not be refunded.

  • Accommodation will be provided from 20th night to 21nd evening. Students should vacate the rooms before the evening of 21st September 2018. Please plan your travel accordingly.

How to reach SSN ?



ROUTE 1 - EGMORE RAILWAY STATION

Egmore Station to SSN college of Engineering

For those who are coming from South Tamil Nadu, it is informed that all the trains going to Chennai Egmore station stop at Tambaram. Tambaram bus stand is adjacent to Tambaram Railway station. Those who are coming from south Tamil Nadu by bus also can get down at Perungulathur and then they can cross the road to reach the bus stop and take the following buses:

Bus Number : 555, 55K & 566 â€“ Thirupporur

 Bus Number : 515 & 115 - Mamallapuram

These buses will reach SSN college of Engineering via Tambaram and Perungulathur. It takes about 45-55 minutes to reach SSN College.

ROUTE 2 - CENTRAL RAILWAY STATION

Broadway to SSN College of Engineering.

Those who are coming from north Tamil Nadu can use this route. At the gate of Chennai Central Railway station, you can board a bus to Broadway. Normally all the buses entering the central station and stopping at the bay will go to Broadway bus stand. It takes 4-6 minutes to reach Broadway.

At Broadway bus stand you can take the following route buses to Kelambakkam (which is about 4 kms. from the college).

Bus Number : 21H and 21H AC - Kelambakkam

Bus Number : 521- Thirupporur

It takes 1hr 30 minutes to reach Kelambakkam from Broadway. From Kelambakkam the student can board any bus going towards Thirupporur / Mamallapuram and get down at SSN college of Engineering.

ROUTE 3 - KOYAMBEDU BUS TERMINAL

Koyambedu to SSN College of Engineering

Those who are coming from north Tamil Nadu by bus can use this route. At Koyambedu bus stand there are two systems - One is for mofussil bus and the other one for local bus.From Koyembedu Mofussil bus Stand you can board the bus (Route no 119) to Kalppakkam/Mamallapuram, which goes via SSN College of Engineering. For boarding local bus, that is just outside of the Koyembedu bus stand you can board the bus (Route no 570) to Kelambakkam.

Bus Number : 119 - Kalpakkam/Mamallapuram

Bus Number : 570X- ThiruPorur

Both the buses cover the distance about 1.30 hrs to 2 hrs depending on the traffic.
